This course is about understanding and applying modern AI well enough to actually build with it. There are a few things I want you to walk away with.

## How Neural Networks Work

Neural networks are the foundation of most modern AI. A lot of things click into place once you have a real mental model for what's happening inside one. We won't go deep into the math, but you'll come away with enough intuition to reason about why these systems behave the way they do.

## Customizing AI Models

How do you take an existing model (a large language model, a computer vision model, etc.) and tune it to do what you actually need? We'll work with real training and fine-tuning tools. You'll build your own custom computer vision applications and get hands-on with the process.

## AI Services and APIs

There are a huge number of AI services and APIs available right now. Part of this course is exposure to what out there, how it works, and how you pull it into an application. We'll work through this using several platforms including Azure, which has a broad set of AI capabilities and a generous free tier for students.

## Coding with AI

AI is changing how software gets written. You're software developers, and this is directly relevant to your work. We'll build real AI-assisted and AI-first coding workflows, focus on getting quality output from AI tools, and work on keeping projects from falling apart as scope grows.

## Why This Matters

This is the most interesting moment in tech in a long time. You have access to tools and capabilities that simply didn't exist a few years ago. You can build things now, on your own, that would have required a large team before. The job market has real instability, and it also has real opportunity. If you take this seriously and get good at using these tools, there is a lot you can do.

## What You'll Be Doing in Class

Most class time is spent working through guided activities with a partner. You'll explore interactive demos, build small experiments, and work through discussion questions together. Occasionally there will be a short quiz or knowledge check at the start of class. Later in the quarter there will be studio time to work on the final project.

Most instruction is delivered async through readings, videos, and interactive exercises.
