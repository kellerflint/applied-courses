The AI landscape  Every inner term is also the outer one.
Artificial Intelligence
Any technique that makes a machine behave intelligently — including rule-based systems, search algorithms, and learning from data.
Rule-based AI Logic written by hand: decision trees, expert systems, game pathfinding. No learning from data.
Machine Learning
The machine learns patterns from data instead of following hand-coded rules.
fraud detection price prediction spam filtering recommendation engines
Neural Networks
A specific ML approach using layers of connected nodes that learn by adjusting weights.
image recognition voice assistants medical imaging
Deep Learning
Neural networks with many layers, capable of learning very complex patterns.
self-driving cars protein folding real-time translation
Generative AI
Deep learning models that create new content — text, images, audio, video.
ChatGPT DALL-E Suno Midjourney Gemini

Every label in an inner box also applies to the outer boxes. ChatGPT is generative AI, deep learning, a neural network, machine learning, and AI — all at once.
